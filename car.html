Car Sales

Tools: Power BI

Introduction:

The Car Sales Dashboard is designed to provide comprehensive insights into the sales performance of our car dealership. Utilizing Power BI, this interactive dashboard offers real-time visualization of critical Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) related to car sales. The objective is to enable data-driven decision-making by presenting a clear view of sales trends, average prices, and detailed metrics across various dimensions such as body style, color, and dealer regions. This dashboard helps in monitoring progress, identifying growth opportunities, and understanding sales performance over time.

Dashboard Details:

Home:

The Home Page provides a broad overview of key sales metrics and trends, including Year-to-Date (YTD), Month-to-Date (MTD), and Month-over-Month (MoM) figures for total sales, average price, and cars sold. It features visualizations that compare sales by month, week, color, and body style, offering insights into sales distribution and performance over time.

Details:

The Details Page offers an in-depth view of the car sales data, including detailed tables that display comprehensive sales information such as car model, body style, color, sales amount, dealer region, and date. Additionally, it highlights the top 5 regions and top 5 companies by sales, providing a focused view of top performers.
